Experience Details

Detailed Itinerary
1

Morning Departure (9:00 AM)

Meet at Funchal Marina. Brief safety orientation and equipment check before boarding our comfortable minibus.

2

Cabo Girão Skywalk (10:30 AM)

Experience Europe's highest sea cliff with breathtaking views from the glass platform 580 meters above sea level.

3

Levada Walk (12:00 PM)

Gentle 2-hour walk along traditional irrigation channels through laurel forest, with stunning mountain and ocean views.

4

Traditional Lunch & Return (3:00 PM)

Enjoy authentic Madeiran cuisine at a local restaurant, followed by scenic drive back to Funchal (arrival 5:00 PM).

Important Notes
This tour requires a moderate level of fitness. The levada walk involves uneven terrain and some narrow paths.
Weather conditions may affect the itinerary. Alternative routes will be provided if necessary for safety.
